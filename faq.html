Frequently Asked Questions

Can I restrict the number of deliveries for each delivery date?

Yes, you can restrict the number of deliveries per delivery date. You can set number of deliveries in the "Maximum deliveries per day" field on the product delivery date metabox on WooCommerce edit page. The delivery date will be displayed for further deliveries in the calendar after these number of orders.
